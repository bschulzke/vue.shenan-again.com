Shenan-Again — Rulebook

Welcome to Shenan-Again: the Time-Loop Heist RPG

Grab some paper, pencils, a handful of dice, and download the free rulebook below

We won't charge you a dime for the book. That's right, the game is free! We want as many people as possible to play. We do have one request, though.

If you like the game, choose some way to pay it forward. What that means is up to you, but we have a few suggestions to get you started. We're excited to get this game out of our heads and onto your table!
